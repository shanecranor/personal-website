Shane Zachary Cranor

20-21 Months

Shane started the new year by going night night at 7:30 pm and missing the ball drop. He was very excited later that week when Mom got her new Powerbook G4 laptop. "Apple 'puter light on!" he says as he points to the glowing apple icon on the lid. Shane likes to watch slide shows on the computer. He recognizes most of his relatives and their respective pets, as well as some of the animals he saw at the zoo. "I want picture Uncle Jere," he says. He is also learning the letters of the alphabet. He likes to pick them out on the computer keyboard and to look at the big letters Mom shows him on the screen.

The basement construction is almost done. Mom and Dad have been busy painting -- they have gone through many gallons of "vanilla icecream" paint.. Shane likes to go down to the basement to inspect their work. He also likes to help sweep the floor. "Cleaning the letter O," says Shane. This weekend Shane played with Mom and Dad's friend Brian when he came for a visit. Shane also had a breakfast date with his friend Mira. Shane checked out the new Walmart that opened this week and Mom bought him his very own Elmo.

Mom and Dad bought Shane a big boy chair at Ikea. Shane likes it much better than his highchair. He likes to sit in his chair and eat his breakfast. We have gotten lots of snow this winter. Shane doesn't really like going outside in the cold, but he let Mom take him on the sled in the backyard. Shane couldn't believe how much snow we got during the blizzard. He could only walk along the path that Mom and Dad cleared. Shane thinks it is more fun to stay inside where it is warm. He really likes playing in the newly finished basement.

Shane likes to talk a lot. Sometimes he finds himself very funny. He likes to sit on the steps with Mama and eat a snack.
