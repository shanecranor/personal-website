Shane Zachary Cranor

24-25 Months

Shane had lots of guests for his second birthday. Grandma Judy and Grandpa Mike came the day before and brought Shane a climber with a slide. Grandma Connie, Grampa Morris, Aunt Ginny, Uncle Jeff, Justin, Great Granma Gertie, and Great Grandpa Harry came for Shane's party. Shane had an ice cream cake and opened lots of presents.

Shane likes to sing lots of songs. He especially likes to sing when he is supposed to be going night night. The ABC song, the itsy bitsy spider song, and Old MacDonald are among his favorites. Shane went on an airplane with Mom and Dad and flew to Pittsburgh. Shane liked Pittsburgh because they have lots of bridges and choo choos. He especially liked riding the incline.

Shane likes balls. He likes to kick balls, and bounce balls, and sometimes he likes to sit on balls. In gym class he learned how to sit on a ball and do a flop and plop.

Shane likes to go to Panera with Mom and Dad on Saturday mornings after gym class. Sometimes he eats a plain bagel and drinks a "moo cow milk." Wherever he goes, Shane likes to bring his Bob the Builder backpack, loaded up with a few crayons, his school bus, his mini van, some snacks, and a few necessities. When we went to Pittsburgh we accidently left the mini van and school bus in the rental car and we had to call up Hertz and get them to FedEx them back to us.

Shane likes to play on his slide. It is also fun to play with grass and sticks and dirt. Shane got to meet Dad's friends Luke and Mel and Ryan. Ryan likes to turn on light switches too. Shane likes to hug his Tyler doggy. He says "I'm woofing Tyler doggy."
