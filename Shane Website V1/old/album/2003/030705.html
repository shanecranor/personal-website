Shane Zachary Cranor

26-27 Months

Shane went to Hopkinsville, Kentucky with Mom and Dad. He likes to ride the monorail choo choo at Newark airport and help with the suitcases. He is especially concerned about the big black suitcase because he knows that Tyler doggie travels in there. Shane had lots of cousins to play with in Kentucky. Aunt Ginny and Uncle Jeff took Shane swimming in the hotel pool. Shane says he likes to go "this way and that way." Sometimes he likes to pretend he is the hungry caterpillar.

Shane now sleeps in his blue big boy bed. He has a new chest of drawers and lamps for his blue room too. Grandma Connie sent Shane some of the clothes that Dad wore when he was two. Shane tried them on, but he doesn't think they are very comfortable to play in.

Shane went to Maryland to visit Grandma Judy and Grandpa Mike. He went to Grandma Judy's office and saw the geese and the dinosaur. On Saturday he went to Justin's second birthday party. On Sunday lots of relatives came over for Aunt Nancy's birthday. Alex and Mark showed Shane how to paint with water colors. Shane tried on some of Grandma Gladys' jewelry. Amy read Shane a book. Later Shane went to Cabin John Park to ride the train. On Monday Shane went to Justin's house again and played with Justin's new toys.

Shane likes to help Dad take out the garbage. He also likes to watch Dad mow the lawn with his big tractor. Shane pretends his orange car is a tractor. After mowing the lawn, everyone needs to take a bath. Shane likes to eat vanilla yogurt for breakfast. Sometimes he eats lemon yogurt too. But he doesn't like any yogurt that has "crumbs" in it. Shane likes to get mail -- especially presents in the mail. Grandma Connie sent him some new PJs but its too hot to wear them right now.

Watch a movie of Shane at 2 1/4 putting on his sandals and talking about mowing the lawn. "What am I doing?" he asks. "I'm putting them on slowly," he says (he really means "loosely"). If you are not sure what he's saying, read the transcript.
