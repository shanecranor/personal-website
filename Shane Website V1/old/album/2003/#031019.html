Shane Zachary Cranor

28-29 Months

Labor Day weekend was lots of fun. Grandma Connie and Grandpa Morris stopped by for a visit. The big hole in our front yard got filled in with lots of dirt (a process involving dump trucks and backhoes). And Shane went to a party and got to splash in a very big puddle.

Shane got to meet his baby sister Maya. Shane checked her feet to see if she was wearing any shoes. No shoes, but Shane found big toes and little toes!

Shane was very excited when Maya and Mom came home from the hospital. With all his grandparents there too he was really bouncing off the walls. He helped Dad and Grandpa Morris clean up the big tree branch that fell in our backyard during Hurricane Isabel.

Shane is curious about Maya but doesn't want to get too close. Mom signed him up for lots of classes to help get some of the ants out of his pants. He goes to gym class, yoga class, music class, and story time at the library. Shane's friend Mira has a new baby brother named Nathan who is one week younger than Maya. Shane went to the playground with Mira after Nathan's bris.
