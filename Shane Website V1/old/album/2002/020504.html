Shane Zachary Cranor

One Year

All of Shane's grandparents and some of his great grandparents came to visit for his first birthday. Shane got lots of nice presents. Grandma Connie baked Shane a cake, which he enjoyed quite a bit. Dad turned Shane's car seat around so he can face forward now and better enjoy the ride in Mom and Dad's new car.

Mom and Dad got tired of people confusing Shane for a little girl, so they took him for his first hair cut. Now he looks like a little boy again.

Shane likes to go out to eat at restaurants that have balloons. He also likes to eat rice and black beans at Mexican restaurants. Mom and Dad bought Shane some shoes -- size 4 1/2 wide. Shane likes his new shoes. Shane likes to walk pushing his cart or holding somebody's hand. Sometimes he walks a few steps on his own. Walking outside is especially fun.

Shane likes to say "the car, the car, the car!" He also likes to ride in the car. He goes to the front door and says "Mama, go go bye bye!" when he wants to go outside. He has started walking more, and he likes to stop and play in the dirt and pick up sticks. Some of Shane's other new words include: Night night, PJs, nose, tree, bird, doggy, cup, book, ball, and shoes. He tries to say banana too.
