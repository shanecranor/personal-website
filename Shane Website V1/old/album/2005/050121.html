Shane at 44 to 49 months

Shane and Maya have been having a good time riding around on Maya's new Bobby car. Some of their other toys have been put away so they don't get dusty during our great house rewiring project. Shane got lots of holes in the walls and ceiling of his room and a new light fixture. Shane made lots of valentines for his friends and relatives.

Shane had a good time at Harrison's birthday party at the Children's Museum. The water room was an especially big hit. Shane also enjoyed making a print in the art room.

Maya and Shane really like to take baths. Shane had a good time showing everyone his school on Family Day.

Maya likes to wear her backpack around the house, especially when Shane has his backpack on too. Sometimes when Shane is really tired he takes a long nap. Maya thinks it is fun to crawl into bed with him and wake him up.

Shane had a good time collecting eggs at the Smiths' "egg party." Maya and Shane finally got their rooms painted and put back together.

Shane likes the party hat and sunglasses he got at Katheryne's birthday party. He likes to wear them when he eats ravioli -- one of his favorite foods to have for lunch. Shane had a good time when his grandparents and great grandparents came for a visit.

Shane had a great time at the CMU Carnival. He was especially looking forward to riding the purple ride. He remembered riding it last year, but this year he wanted to sit in the driver's seat. A little girl beat him to it, but she was willing to share.

Shane likes the flowers that are growing in our garden. He is learning the names of some of the flowers he sees on the way to school: tulips, daffodils, and hyacinths. He thinks we should plant more tulips in the fall. He is jealous of Maya's poncho -- he wants one too!

Shane went to a sedar at Serge's house. He liked eating the matza.

Shane helped mom bake banana muffins to bring to school. Shane helped dad bake a birthday cake.

Mom and Dad came to Shane's school on his birthday and brought the banana muffins. Mom read Green Eggs and Ham to the class. The Smith's came over for dinner and had pizza and birthday cake. Shane enjoyed unwrapping his presents.

Shane likes to look at all the flowers growing in our garden. He knows the names of some of the flowers now. He thinks we should plant tulips in more colors next fall.

Shane watched the Race for the Cure race through our neighborhood. He likes the new paint in the hallway (used to be green). Shane had a good time at the SCS/ECE picnic.

Shane had a good time visiting Mom and Dad's new offices in the CIC building.

Shane went to Maryland to visit Grandma Judy and Papa Mike. He had a good time playing with cousins Justin and Eric.

Shane and Maya had a fun time at the Pittsburgh Science Center.

Shane and Maya like to pretend Shane's baskets are boats and scoot around Shane's room.

Shane had a good time at Jack's birthday party, but it was very hot outside. Shane got a very loud horn at Jack's party. Mom and Dad will only let him play it outside.
