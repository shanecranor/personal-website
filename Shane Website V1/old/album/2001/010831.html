Shane's Eighteenth Week

Labor Day Weekend: Shane got a swing last week -- now he has a place to sit at the kitchen table. Shane went with Mom and Dad to Panera for lunch and dad got an ice tea in this big cup. Shane was impressed that Dad could finish off three of these! Shane tried on the sweater Great Grandma Gertie knit for him, but it's still too hot for sweaters. On Monday Shane had lunch with Mira and her parents.

Shane's Nineteenth Week

Shane went with Mom and Dad to Grandma Gertie and Grandpa Harry's house on Saturday. Grandma babysat while Mom and Dad went to a wedding. On Sunday Grandma Connie and Grandpa Morris and Great Grandma Ginnie came to visit. Shane has gotten very good at rolling over and he likes to spend time on his tummy looking around. He's also into blowing bubbles and saying "Bbbbbb."

On September 11 Mom flew to Washington, DC. After the terrorist attacks, she couldn't fly home or even take the train. So Grandma Judy and Grandpa Mike drove her to Deleware. Dad and Shane and Yvonned met Mom at Grandma Connie and Granpa Morris' house.

Shane's Twentieth Week

The weather is getting cooler, so Shane wears a hat when he goes for his evening walk. Mom got a new sling that's very colorful. Shane is learning how to sit, but he still needs some help to keep from toppeling over.
