Shane's Fifteenth and Sixteenth Weeks

Shane went to Maryland to visit Grandma Judy and Grandpa Mike. He met his cousin Justin too, and saw his Uncle Jeremy and Auntie Amber and their dog Jalapeno. He also helped Great Grandpa Bob celebrate his 80th birthday.

Shane went to Hopkinsville, Kentucky to visit Great Grandma Vivian and Great Grandpa Buddy, and Great Grandma Virginia. He also got to see Cousins Tim and Theresa and Ross and ride on their house boat. Great Aunt Kathy and Great Great Aunt Jesse also visited.

Shane's Seventeenth Week

After all the excitement of the previous two weeks, Shane stayed home and worked on rolling over and pushing up on his arms. Shane is now 14.5 pounds and 26 inches long. That's pretty long for such a little fellow.
