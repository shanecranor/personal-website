Shane Zachary Cranor

Shane's Twenty-eighth Week

Shane flew to Denver with mom and dad. They travelled to Keystone with Grandma Connie and Grandpa Morris. Shane is starting to babble. In addition, to "eh," he now says "ah" and "bah" and "ah-bah." And when he's mad he says "um" and "ma." And when he's happy he likes to giggle and shriek.

Shane's Twenty-ninth Week

Shane enjoyed going out to dinner with everyone, and especially sitting in the high chair. He saw the continental divide at the Loveland Pass and Buffalo Bill's grave in Golden. On Thanksgiving Shane went to Pennsylvania to visit (Great) Aunt Bonnie and Uncle Fred. He saw Great Grandma Gertie and Great Grandpa Harry there too. And he met Shayna the dog. Shane was happy to meet Shayna, but Shayna wanted to run away.

Shane's Thirtieth Week

After Thanksgiving, Shane got to meet Gopal, who was visiting from California.

Shane's Thirty-first Week

Shane likes to eat lots of fruits and vegetables. He especially likes the sweet fruits. He is developing a sweet tooth, but he doesn't have any teetch yet. Mommy made Shane some homemade sweet potatoes. Shane made quite a mess when he ate them, so he took a bath in the sink to clean up (the third sink photo was featured on our holiday cards). Shane has become quite a good crawler. He can now climb the two steps up from the family room into the kitchen and also pull himself up to a standing position when he holds onto something, but he still falls down a lot because his balance isn't very good. This week Shane visited with cousin Neil and Jacqueline.

Shane's Thirty-second Week

Shane likes to play with Dad's laptop computer. He also likes to find all the ethernet cables in the house. Peas are his favorite green vegetable -- much better than green beans! Shane celebrated his first Hanukah. He really liked the candles and the pretty ribbons on all the packages.
