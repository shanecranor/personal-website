Shane's Thirty-third Week

Shane likes to go to Panera with Mom and Dad -- Panera is just like the St. Louis Bread Company, which Mom and Dad used to go to all the time when they lived in St. Louis.

Shane's Thirty-fourth Week

Shane had an exciting winter vacation. Before leaving town with Mom and Dad he got a visit from Mira and Anna. Then he went to Maryland (by way of Deleware) and visited Grandma Judy and Grandpa Mike. Uncle Jeremy and Auntie Amber were there too, along with Jal the dog. The whole gang (except Jal) wrapped presents on Christmas Eve to raise money for the Juvenile Diabetes Foundation. Shane enjoyed playing with the ribbons until he fell asleep. Then everyone went out for Chinese food afterwards. On Christmas Day Shane went to Aunt Ginny and Uncle Jeff and Cousin Justin's house. Shane got to meet Tyler the dog. Grandma Connie and Grandpa Morris were there too. There were lots of presents for everyone -- so many that Shane fell asleep before they were all unwrapped.

Shane's Thirty-fifth Week

After Shane got back from Maryland it was a little boring because there weren't so many people around to play with. But fortunatly he has lots of toys to play with. And Shane was busy working on his climbing skills too. Then two days later all of Shane's grandparents and Amber and Jeremy and Jal came to visit him. Then there was New Year's Eve and Shane got to stay up late and watch the ball drop on TV. The next day a whole bunch of Mom and Dad's friends from grad school came to visit -- Milind, Zubin, Christos, Diana, and Apostolos.
