Shane at 57-60 Months

Shane and Maya like to play silly games in the kitchen. Playing in the snow if fun too. Shane likes to play with fabric scraps in Mom's sewing room.

Shane goes to Gymkhanna on Saturday mornings.

Shane is starting to tell stories in his artwork. This painting has three trees and a rocket going to Pluto.

Shane and Maya and Dad helped Mom make a belly cast.

Shane likes to race around the house with his shopping cart. He also likes to dance in the family room. Some of his favorite songs to dance to are Choo Choo Mama, Yellow River, and Holly Hop.

Shane and Maya like to play in the sandbox with Davis, Cole, and Molly. Shane got to try out bowling at Kathryn's birthday party.

Shane and Maya like to blow bubbles.

Shane's baby sister Nina was born on April 13. She came home on Easter Sunday and the neighborhood kids had an Easter egg hunt in our yard. All of Shane's grandparents came to visit. Papa Morris has been walking Shane to school.

Three car seats just barely fit in the back of our car. Shane is glad he gets to sit next to Nina.

Shane had his first baseball practice with the Squirrel Hill Baseball pre-school Junior Pirates. Several of his friends are on his team.

Shane likes to hold Nina and talk to her.

Shane had his fifth birthday party at Gymkhana.
